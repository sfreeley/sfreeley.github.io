trackRx

Front-end Capstone Project

Inspired by my experience as a pharmacist, and understanding that patients have a very difficult time managing multiple medications for multiple disease states, as well as understanding the reasons behind taking certain medications, and making sense of large amounts of information coming from their medical providers and pharmacists, this application sought to help alleviate some of this confusion. Users can:

- Document the drug name, dosage, dosage form, their patient specific directions, indication, and patient specific notes all in one place.
- Input prescription numbers and refills (if applicable) as well as last fill date and days supply, which will then automatically calculate their next refill date.
- Track next refill dates for each medication with a real-time countdown (by days), which will also be displayed to prevent users from running out of their medication.
- Utilize complete CRUD functionality and perform each of these functions on each medication entry.
- Search specific keywords pertaining to their medications (ie name, dosage, dosage form, indication, etc)
